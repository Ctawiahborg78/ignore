Job Specification

Job Title: Web Developer

Location: Virtual

Type: Full-time

Job Description

Hot Beans Web is a web development company with a passion for creating innovative and intuitive web solutions. We're looking for a talented and enthusiastic web developer to join our team. You will be responsible for developing and maintaining websites for our clients in this role. You will work closely with our project managers and designers to ensure that our websites are delivered on time and to the highest quality.

Responsibilities:

  • Design and develop high-quality websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and other web development languages and frameworks
  • Collaborate with project managers and designers to ensure that websites meet client requirements and are delivered on time
  • Troubleshoot and debug web development issues as needed
  • Stay up-to-date with

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field, or equivalent experience
  • Strong knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Experience with other web development languages and frameworks such as PHP, MySQL, and AngularJS
  • Excellent communication and problem-solving skills
  • Ability to work independently and collaboratively
  • Strong attention to detail and ability to meet deadlines

Benefits

  • Competitive salary and benefits package
  • Flexible work schedule
  • Opportunities for growth and development within the company
